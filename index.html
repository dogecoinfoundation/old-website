Serving the Dogecoin Community.

The Dogecoin Foundation is a non-profit organization created by the Dogecoin community, for the Dogecoin community, to facilitate the use of Dogecoin through goodwill, promotional and charitable endeavors.

So whether there is a project that you would like to see happen or you just want to spread the love using the magic of doge, we invite you to work with us.

Donating to Charities and Funding Causes.

The Dogecoin community, as you might know, is a friendly bunch of people. As a community, we make massive efforts to help people whether they're on the moon or on Earth. That's just our nature.

Promoting the use of Dogecoin - our fun currency.

We all wish Dogecoin will reach the moon, may that day be sooner or later. But it all starts with what us, the community, can do.
