Serving the Dogecoin Community.

The Dogecoin Foundation is a non-profit organization created by the founders of Dogecoin for the purpose of spreading the use of the currency through goodwill and charitable endeavors.

So whether there is a project that you would like to see happen or you just want to spread the love using the magic of doge, please consider making a dogenation today.

Donating to Charities and Funding Causes.

The Dogecoin Community as you know is a friendly bunch of people. As a community, we make massive efforts to help people whether they're on the moon or on Earth. That's just our nature.

What Dogecoin does is magnificent, it's amazing. So, what does the Dogecoin Foundation do? One job the Dogecoin Foundation does is that we find and publicize charities for shibes to help. The rest is history.

Promoting the use of Dogecoin - our fun currency.

We all wish Dogecoin will reach the moon, may that day be sooner or later. But it all starts with what us, the community, can do.

The volunteers at the Dogecoin Foundation help promote Dogecoin, so more and more people know about it and we can get it in the hands of more shibes. Think of us as the "backend" of the Dogecoin community.
