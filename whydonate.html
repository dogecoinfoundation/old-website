Very gratitude. So helping. Wow.


There are many reasons you may wish to donate and we will make sure your donation goes towards exactly the purpose you want it to.

Donate To Charity

Our list of charitable organizations have been vetted for legitmacy so you can be sure your hard earned doge is going to a good cause.

Add To A Development Bounty

A donation towards a bounty is both a vote to see something developed and the creation of an incentive for a developer to work on that project. Contributing to a bounty also earns you the right to request specific features that you want to see included!

Infrastructure Support

Many great people are hosting critical but expensive infrastructure while gaining no financial reward for themselves, and who are often unknown to the everyday user. We want to help supplement these expenses so that they can carry on doing the great work that they do.

Contribute To An Outreach Fund

Help promote the dogecoin brand by giving coins towards sponsorship programs such as community competitions, esports tournament prizes and other events with a high potential user base.

Sharing The Love

If you don't have any specific preference on where you want you donations to go, we will make sure we distribute it to where it's needed most and you can sleep with the warm fuzzy feeling of knowing that your contribution is helping dogecoin to reach the moon.

See our list of initiatives to find out more!
